Simplifications of simplified blackjack

I Dealing cards is all at once per player rather than one card per player per dealing round.
II Player cannot view one of the dealer's cards.
III Player cannot split.
IV Aces ONLY count as 11.
V Blackjack does not beat 21. (2 card 21 vs. 3+ card 21)
VI Corollary to V: Dealer does not stop play on blackjack.
VII Dealer wins pushes.
VIII Player cannot wager.
IX Corollary to VIII: Player cannot double down, surrender, or take insurance.
